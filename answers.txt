Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: For straightforward version control, especially in collaboration
with other people who are working on the same project simultaneously.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: We can apply data science to social science by gathering large amounts of information about people
and analyzing the trends among them in order to reveal societal patterns, like with a large election
survey.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging applies the changes of a current branch to the main branch,
preserving the history and combining them both into one. Rebasing just
"replays" the sequence of edits from the main branch to the current
branch, as if they happened in sequence from the main branch originally.
